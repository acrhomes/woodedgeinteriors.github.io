About US

We provide our services exclusively in HYDERABAD , VISAKHAPATNAM

Woodedge Interiors, in collaboration with ACR HOMES, stands at the forefront of innovative construction, design, painting, and rebuilding services. With a commitment to excellence and a passion for transforming spaces, we redefine interior aesthetics and functionality.

Construction: Our construction services encompass a wide spectrum, from residential to commercial projects. Whether it's creating bespoke homes, renovating office spaces, or constructing retail outlets, we excel in bringing architectural visions to life. With a focus on quality craftsmanship and attention to detail, we ensure that every structure we build meets the highest standards of durability and design.

Designing: At Woodedge Interiors, design is not just about aesthetics; it's about creating environments that enhance living and working experiences. Our team of talented designers works closely with clients to understand their unique needs and preferences, crafting personalized spaces that reflect their style and personality. From conceptualization to execution, we integrate creativity with functionality to deliver spaces that inspire and delight.


Rebuilding: Rebuilding is about revitalizing existing structures, breathing new life into outdated spaces, and maximizing their potential. Our rebuilding services encompass everything from structural renovations to interior makeovers, aimed at enhancing functionality, efficiency, and aesthetic appeal. Whether it's restoring historic buildings or modernizing outdated interiors, we approach every project with a commitment to excellence and a dedication to exceeding client expectations.


Together, Woodedge Interiors and ACR HOMES form a powerhouse of expertise and innovation in the realm of construction, design, painting, and rebuilding. With a shared vision of creating spaces that inspire and endure, we are dedicated to transforming dreams into reality.
